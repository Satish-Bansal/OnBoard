background

OnBoard is a patented Bus Identification and Navigation System developed by IIT Delhi to assist visually impaired users in boarding the correct bus. OnBoard is licensed to RLF for global implementation.

The system has been successfully tested in Delhi and Mumbai on multiple bus routes, and now being deployed in other cities.

For this innovation Prof. M. Balakrishnan & Prof. P.V.M. Rao received a National Award from the Department of Science and Technology (DST).

The device solves two challenges faced by persons with visual impairment

Helps identify the route number of the bus

Helps identify the location the bus door
